Multimedia and Information Security Lab (MIS Lab) is established by
Dr. K. V. Arya in 2011 for global excellence in the filed of multimedia
and secure information processing.

The lab is set to create a perfect professional environment for students of IPG, M.Tech., and Ph.D. to pursue their research and studies in the area of image processing, video processing, biometrics, network, information security etc.

Multimedia and Information Security Lab is air-conditioned and well-furnished, with 25 computer systems having latest Intel Core i3/i5/i7 processors with LAN as well as WAN connectivity.In addition the lab provides high speed wifi support which enhances
the network environment in the MIS Lab.

Systems in lab are Window as well as Linux based which gives a hands-on exposure to students on both the operating systems. The lab is equipped with software packages
like MATLAB, Rattle as well as with network simulator like NS2/NS3, OPNET etc. The students have the privilege to utilize the lab without any time constraints which makes the MIS lab an excellent research center.

The MIS Lab has collaboration with foreign universities such as San Diago State University, San Diago, USA and City University London, UK to give the students an international exposure in their research work. The lab provides a coherent environment for academic discussion among the student working in these areas.
