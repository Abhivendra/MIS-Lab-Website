‘Global Excellence in Multimedia and Secure Information Processing”

Vision :

• A recognized world class center of academic research and technological excellence in Information security and multimedia.


• A knowledge sharing center for inter-disciplinary researchers under a unique umbrella.

Mission :

• To create experts to strengthen global information security. Multimedia and information security engages both students as well as faculty in real world applied research.


• To conduct both theoretical and applied research in the area of multimedia and information security.


• To explore and develop theoretical foundations and apply them in innovative ways to secure the information infrastructure.


• To cater the laboratory need of following courses: Network Management System, Computer Vision, and Information Privacy and Computer Security.
