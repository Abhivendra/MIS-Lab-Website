About

Saqib has done Masters degree in Computer Science with Information Security as specialization from ABV-IIITM Gwalior India in 2012. Cur- rently, he is working at Samsung Research Institute, Noida as software developer. He has previously worked in Aricent Group as a software engineer. His research interests include Network Security, Information Security, Penetration Testing, Android System and System C. He is an Offensive Security Certified Professional and PRISM certified profes- sional by Aricent Group.

Message

Software is ubiquitous in the twenty-first century, ranging from games to stock market. Thus software security has never been more important than now, especially when system breaches have also become increasingly frequent. There was dearth of facilities and opportunities in the institute as no dedicated lab in the institute had the facility to perform practical related to Information security field. This led me to initiate talks with Dr. K. V. Arya about setting up of a dedicated and full-fledged lab of information security within the institute premise. With the positive support from administration, we were finally able to setup "Multimedia & Information Security Lab". There are 25 PCs were installed in this lab having secluded secure network. In addition to that we installed information security OS backtrack 5 R1 is installed on all system and one server was set up in lab to monitor all the activities in the lab. The lab provided hands on practice on Information secu- rity tools such as Metaspoilt Framework, IDS( snort),Wireshark, TCP dump, Arppoof, etc. As penetration testing is one of the most impor- tant part of security module, we also organized workshop on Penetration Testing with Backtrack 5 was also conducted in the lab. I am certain that, with the establishment of this lab, now students can explore more in the field of Information Security field i.e. Penetra- tion Testing, Information Security Analyst, etc. Also, as students can get practical exposure with the help of accessories and facilities inside the lab, they can also opt for various reputed IS certification exams like OSCP, CEH, CISSP, etc.

About

Sumit received his Master's degree in Computer Science with Informa- tion Security as specialization from IIIT Gwalior India in 2012. He is working in Device Security Assurance Center, ABB Global Industries and Services Limited, Bangalore as Cyber Security Analyst. He has previously worked in Directi Internet Solutions Pvt. Ltd as a System Administrator. His research interests include Network Security, Infor- mation Security, SCADA Protocols and Python. He is an Offensive Security Certified Professional and Offensive Security Certified Expert. He has three papers to his credit.

Message

Lab is an essential part of every R & D activity and I realised this in institute when the students of information security field were not able to test applications practically. To explore more in this field and to motivate others to join, we founded the lab. Workshops were organised so that students become familiar with working of different tools such as Metasploit framework, IDS (snort), Wireshark, TCP dump, ARPspoof etc. Licenses were purchased officially to use various tools. We organized one-day workshop on Metaspoilt framework and also on penetration testing with backtrack 5. It has been very beneficial to the students, as already two students have got OSCP certification by offensive security. Further, this lab will help students to acquire various IS certifications like and will motivate them to learn more in the field of information security to become information security expert.
